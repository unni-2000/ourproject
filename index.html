About

The project intends to promote mental health awareness in IT workforce. This will help employers to understand the mental health level of employees and make necessary changes to their work schedule. Employers can ask the employees to do the survey and based on the results they can categorize their mental health issues.

MACHINE LEARNING ENABLED

Machine learning enhances mental distress prediction with improved accuracy and personalized insights.

REAL TIME RESPONSE

The ability of a system or model to provide immediate and timely predictions or interventions based on continuously updated data.

DATASTORING CAPABILITY

The systems can effectively store and manage the data required for ongoing analysis, model improvement, and potentially contribute to advancing mental health research and intervention strategies.

SCALES

A comprehensive set of scales to accurately assess and predict individual mental distress levels. Through a meticulous process, we utilize widely recognized scales such as the Perceived Stress Scale (PSS), the Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD) scale, and the Patient Health Questionnaire (PHQ).
